Spicy One-Pot Shakshouka

Home

Description

Easy and delicious eggs poached in a spicy tomato sauce. A popular Middle Eastern dish that has become a favorite with my family as well. Don't forget flatbreads or crusty bread for dipping!

You are going to love the burst of Mediterranian flavours melting on your tongue!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Chili Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Heat oil and saute onion and bell peppers
  2. Add chili pepper, garlic, peppers, tomatoes, sugar - season well
  3. Make dents in sauce and crack eggs into em!
  4. Garnish with green onions
